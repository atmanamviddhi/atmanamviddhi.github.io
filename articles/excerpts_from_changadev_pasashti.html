Excerpts from Changadev Pasashti

Changadev Pasashti (Translated by Swami Abhayananda)

18/2/2025
Advait-Vedanta

When He is revealed, the Universe disappears;
When He is concealed, the Universe shines forth.
Yet He doesn’t hide Himself,
Nor does He reveal Himself;
He is always present before us at every moment.

verse: 2

It is not ignorance that causes the separation
Between the perceiver and the perceived;
Truly, everything is Himself,
And He is the cause of everything.

verse: 8

It’s the one pure Consciousness that becomes everything.
From the Gods above to the Earth below.

verse: 12

Though the shadows on the wall are ever changing,
The wall itself remains steady and immobile.
Likewise, the forms of the Universe take shape.
Upon the one eternal and unchanging Consciousness.

verse: 13

From within Its own divine pure depths,
It gives birth to the perceivable world.
The perceiver, the perceived, and the act of perception:
These three form the eternal triad of manifestation.
