Excerpts from **Shivastotravali of Acharya Utpaladeva** Sahaj Vichar Vol. 3 translation by Dina Nath Ganjoo.

**ध्यायतो न जपतः स्याद्यस्याविधिपूर्वकम् ।\
एवमेव शिवाभासस्तं नुमो भक्तिशालिनम् ॥१॥**

| Sanskrit Word | English Meaning | 
|:--------------:|:---------------:|
| ध्यायत् | meditating |
| जप | muttering prayers |
| स्यद | speed / rapid motion |
| एवमेव | just / similarly |
| भासस् | lustre / light |
| शालिन् | praiseworthy |
{:.table-customised}

I bow to the lustrous devotee, \
Who without meditation, or muttering phrases\
Spontaneously just like that, \
Realized the auspicious self. — Shiva (1.1)

---

**लब्धत्वत्संपदा भक्तिमतां त्वत्पुरवासिनाम् । \
सञ्चारो लोकमार्गेऽपि स्तात्तयैव विजृभ्भया ॥३॥**

| Sanskrit Word | English Meaning | 
|:--------------:|:---------------:|
| लब्ध | gained / recieved |
| सम्पद | wealth |
| भक्तिमत् | accompanied by devotion or loyalty |
| पुरवासिन् | dwelling in a town |
| सञ्चार | communication |
| लोकमार्ग | general or universal way |
{:.table-customised}

For a devotee bestowed with prosperity of your grace,\
Living in your city - universal consciousness\
O Lord! Even the worldly journey\
Is the same blissful exhilaration. (1.3)

---

**साक्षाद्भवन्मये नाथ सर्वस्मिन् भुवनान्तरे ।\
किंन भक्तिमतां क्षेत्रं मन्त्रः क्कैषां न सिद्धयति ॥४॥**

| Sanskrit Word | English Meaning | 
|:--------------:|:---------------:|
| साक्षाद्भवति | appear personally |
| भवन | residence |
| भुवन | world |
| क्षेत्र | place |
{:.table-customised}

O Lord! For your devotees, \
What place is not sacred? \
What incantation unsuccessful? \
In all these worlds, verily your identity. (1.4)

---

**सर्वतो विलसद्भक्तितेजोध्वस्तावृतेर्मम । \
प्रत्यक्षसर्वभावस्य चिन्तानामापि नश्यतु ॥१९॥**

Having destroyed all obstructions, (of ignorance)\
Through the splendour of enveloping effulgent devotion, \
And realized the true nature of everything, (I request) \
Even traces of anxiety to vanish from me.(1.11)

---

**चित्रं निसर्गतो नाथ दुःखबीजमिदं मनः ।\
त्वद्भक्तिरससंसिक्तं निःश्रेयसमहाफलम् ॥२६॥**

| Sanskrit Word | English Meaning | 
|:--------------:|:---------------:|
| निसर्ग | natural state |
| संसिक्त | well sprinkled or moistened |
| निःश्रेयस | ultimate bliss |
{:.table-customised}

What a wonder, O Lord, \
Mind the seed of only misery, by nature  \
Bears magnificent fruit, blissful tranquillity, \
When drenched in the nectar, “your devotion". (1.26)

---

**परमामृतसान्द्राय शीतलाय शिवाग्नये । \
कस्मैचिद्विश्वसंप्लोषविषमाय नमोऽस्तु ते ॥३॥**

| Sanskrit Word | English Meaning | 
|:--------------:|:---------------:|
| परम | absolute / highest |
| सान्द्र | tender |
| सम्प्लव | destruction |
| विषमायते | become or appear uneven |
{:.table-customised}

Obeisance to Thee! The unique benedictive (Shiva) fire, \
Dreadful, as cause to burn (duality of) the worlds, \
Cool and calm, destroying all woe and worry, \
Tender alluring, super ambrocia—bliss of awareness. (2.3)

---

**महादेवाय रुद्राय शङ्कराय शिवाय ते । \
महेश्वरायापि नमः कस्मैचिन्मन्त्रभूर्तये ॥४॥**

Obeisance to Thee! O God of gods \
The unique identity of “I” conscience \
The Supreme Lord, destroyer of duality \
Bestower of benediction, tranquillity incarnate. (2.4)

---

**नमो निकृत्तनिःशेषत्रैलोक्यविगलद्वसा- \
वसेकविषमायापि मङ्गलाय शिवाग्नये ॥५॥**

| Sanskrit Word | English Meaning | 
|:--------------:|:---------------:|
| निकृत्त | cut up / cut off |
| निःशेष | whole / entire |
| त्रैलोक्य | three worlds |
| विगलति { विगल् } | vanish / disappear |
{:.table-customised}

Obeisance to Thee! The benedictive fire \
Terribly gruesome, accepting as an oblation \
The molten marrow of all the three worlds, cut \
Monom to pieces (dualism) \
But still auspiciousness personified. (2.5)

---
